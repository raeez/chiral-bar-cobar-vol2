\section{Conclusion and Outlook}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We have developed complete rigorous foundations for $A_\infty$ chiral algebras in 3d holomorphic-topological quantum field theories, resolving critical gaps in the literature and establishing precise connections to vertex algebra theory, deformation quantization, and quantum groups.

\subsection{Summary of Main Results}

Our main contributions are:

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\Roman*)]
\item \textbf{Axiomatic Definition} (Section~\ref{sec:foundations}): We constructed the HT operad $\OHT=C_\ast\!\bigl(W(\SCchtop)\bigr)$ and defined $A_\infty$ chiral algebras as algebras over $\OHT$, with explicit sesquilinearity axioms relating $\partial$-equivariance to the $\lambda$-calculus.

\item \textbf{Chain-Level Structure} (Section \ref{sec:FM_calculus}): We proved that bulk local operators form an $A_\infty$ chiral algebra at the chain level, with $A_\infty$ identities arising from Stokes' theorem on FM compactifications and Arnold cancellations at codimension-2 corners.

\item \textbf{PVA Descent} (Section \ref{sec:PVA_descent}): We established that cohomology $H^\bullet(\mathcal{A}, Q)$ is a Poisson vertex algebra by verifying all axioms from Khan--Zeng \cite{KZ25} and proving higher operations $m_{k \geq 3}$ vanish in cohomology via explicit homotopies.

\item \textbf{Worked Examples} (Section \ref{sec:examples_complete}): We computed $A_\infty$ operations explicitly for free chiral multiplets, Landau--Ginzburg models (resolving the $m_{k \geq 4}$ truncation contradiction), and abelian Chern--Simons theory.

\item \textbf{Chiral Hochschild Cohomology} (Section \ref{sec:chiral_hochschild}): We connected bulk observables to chiral Hochschild cochains via dimensional descent from 2d Poisson sigma models and factorization homology on the Ran space.

\item \textbf{Bar--Cobar Duality} (Section \ref{sec:bar_cobar}): We extended Gui--Li--Zeng quadratic duality to the $A_\infty$ setting, proving $d_{\overline{B}}^2 = 0$ using FM--Stokes--Arnold calculus and establishing compatibility with GLZ.

\item \textbf{Line Operators and Yangians} (Section \ref{sec:line_operators}): We proved line operators are modules for the Koszul dual $\mathcal{A}^!$, which carries a dg-shifted Yangian structure with spectral $R$-matrix $r(z)$ satisfying the $A_\infty$ Yang--Baxter equation.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Open Questions and Future Directions}

Several important questions remain open:

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\arabic*)]
\item \textbf{Complete Quantization}: Our results are primarily at tree level and one loop. A systematic analysis of all-loop quantum corrections requires:
\begin{itemize}
\item Renormalization of higher $m_k$ beyond leading order;
\item Proof of convergence/finiteness at all loops under hypotheses H1--H4;
\item Understanding anomaly cancellations in the $d'=1$ formality gap regime.
\end{itemize}

\item \textbf{Non-Perturbative Effects}: Our framework is perturbative. Extensions to include:
\begin{itemize}
\item Instanton contributions (e.g., monopole operators in 3d gauge theories);
\item Non-perturbative line operators (genuine quantum groups, not dg-shifted);
\item S-duality and mirror symmetry transformations.
\end{itemize}

\item \textbf{Higher-Dimensional Generalizations}: The $A_\infty$ chiral algebra structure for $d'=1$ topological directions suggests:
\begin{itemize}
\item $L_\infty$ chiral algebras for $d' \geq 2$ (Lie$_\infty$ structures in place of $A_\infty$);
\item Polysimplicial chiral operads for theories on $\C^n$ with multiple holomorphic directions;
\item Connections to celestial holography and twisted holographic dualities.
\end{itemize}

\item \textbf{Geometric Langlands}: Kapustin--Witten's 4d $\mathcal{N}=4$ theory and its dimensional reductions produce:
\begin{itemize}
\item 3d HT theories related to the geometric Langlands program;
\item $A_\infty$ chiral algebras encoding arithmetic/geometric data (e.g., automorphic forms);
\item Connections to derived algebraic geometry and $\infty$-categorical enhancements.
\end{itemize}

\item \textbf{Applications to Knot Invariants}: Line operators in 3d HT Chern--Simons theory produce:
\begin{itemize}
\item Quantum invariants of knots and links (Jones, HOMFLY, Khovanov homology);
\item Categorical lifts via $\mathcal{A}^!$-modules and derived categories;
\item Connections to skein algebras and cluster algebras.
\end{itemize}
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Broader Impact}

Understanding $A_\infty$ chiral algebras rigorously opens new avenues for applying quantum field theory methods to pure mathematics, including:
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Representation theory}: Modules for $A_\infty$ chiral algebras generalize vertex algebra representations;
\item \textbf{Deformation theory}: Tamarkin's $*$-Lie framework connects to Kontsevich's formality theorems;
\item \textbf{Homological algebra}: Bar--cobar duality provides computational tools for derived categories;
\item \textbf{Mathematical physics}: Precise definitions enable rigorous proofs of physical dualities (3d mirror symmetry, AGT correspondence).
\end{itemize}

Our work establishes $A_\infty$ chiral algebras as a fundamental structure in modern mathematical physics, bridging vertex algebras, factorization algebras, and quantum groups.
