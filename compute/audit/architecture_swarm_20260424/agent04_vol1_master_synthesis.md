## Executive Finding

architecture only after a strict separation of:

1. global BD/CG factorization data on Ran or on the HT product geometry,
2. the local formal-disc shadow encoded by `\mathsf{SC}^{\mathrm{ch,top}}`,

drift: local-shadow statements, binary-degree collapses, or completed

unconditional equivalences.
